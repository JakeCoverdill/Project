JMJ Creations is a family owned business founded by Chris Schoettle, and based in Greenwood Indiana. Chris has a bachelor's degree in Construction Technology and has worked in the Construction Industry for the past 11 years. Chris has been the manager over the construction teams working on the Lucas Oil Stadium, as well as Banker's Life Fieldhouse.

Chris has a very large family support system that keeps his work firmly rooted in his Catholic faith. His family of 8 has a very special devotion to the Blessed Virgin Mary, and pray to her daily. The company name, JMJ Creations, stems from the Holy Family: Jesus, Mary, and Joseph. This devotion drives Chris to be honest and dedicated in all that he does.
